Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert between different time zones?

Simply add the locations you want to compare by using the search bar. The current time in each location will be displayed automatically. You can also adjust the time in any location to see the corresponding times in all other locations.

Does the time zone converter account for Daylight Saving Time?

Yes, our time zone converter automatically accounts for Daylight Saving Time (DST) changes in all supported regions. You'll always get the correct conversion regardless of when DST starts or ends in different locations.

How can I plan a meeting across multiple time zones?

Use our Meeting Planner feature after adding all relevant locations. The color-coded grid shows working hours, off hours, and sleeping hours for each location, making it easy to find suitable meeting times for everyone.

Can I see the time difference between two locations?

Yes, when you add multiple locations, you can easily see the time difference between them. The current time is displayed for each location, making it simple to understand the time difference at a glance.

How do I share my time zone conversions with others?

After setting up your desired time zones, click the "Copy Link" button to generate a shareable URL. Anyone who opens this link will see the exact same time zone configuration you've created.
